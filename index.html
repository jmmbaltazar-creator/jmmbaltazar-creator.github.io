Frequently Asked Questions

✦
When is the RSVP deadline?▾

Please RSVP by June 30th, so we can finalize our arrangements. You may send a message to either Martin or Brenda directly.

Can I bring a plus one?▾

We'd love to celebrate with everyone, but due to limited space, we're only able to accomodate guests named on the invitation.

Are kids welcome?▾

We're keeping the celebration adults-only, with the exception of family members' children. Thank you for understanding!

Is there parking available?▾

Yes! Both the church and reception have ample parking space for your convenience.

Do you have a gift registry?▾

Your presence at our wedding is the greatest gift we could ask for. Should you wish to give something, a monetary gift would be deeply appreciated as we begin our married life together.

Can I take photos during the ceremony?▾

Yes, but please be mindful and allow our dedicated photo and video team to capture key moments, particularly during the procession and bridal entrance.

Is the wedding indoors?▾

Yes, both the ceremony and reception venues are indoors.

What should I wear?▾

Please refer to the Wedding Attire Guide section.
