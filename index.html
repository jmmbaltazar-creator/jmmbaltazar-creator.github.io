Everything you need to know

Frequently Asked
Questions

✦
Is the wedding child-friendly?▾

Children are welcome at the ceremony. The reception dinner is for adults; however, younger members of the immediate family are welcome. We recommend arranging childcare for the evening so parents can celebrate fully.

Where and when should I RSVP?▾

Please RSVP by August 15, 2026. You may reply via email or contact Martin or Brenda directly. Kindly include any dietary requirements or food allergies when you respond so we can make the necessary arrangements.

Is there parking available?▾

Yes! Both St. John Marie Vianney Parish and Charm Hotel & Events Place have available parking on-site. We encourage carpooling with fellow guests when possible.

Will there be transport between the church and reception?▾

The church and reception venue are conveniently close to each other in Silang, Cavite. Guests are welcome to drive or ride together. Details on shuttle arrangements, if any, will be confirmed closer to the date.

Do you have hotel recommendations nearby?▾

Charm Hotel & Events Place offers in-house accommodations for guests who wish to stay overnight. There are also several hotels in Tagaytay (10–15 mins away), including Taal Vista Hotel and various Airbnb options in the area.

Do you have a gift registry?▾

Your presence at our wedding is the greatest gift we could ask for. If you wish to give something, we have a cash gift envelope system available at the reception, as well as a honeymoon fund. Details will be shared at the event.

Can I take photos during the ceremony?▾

We kindly request an unplugged ceremony — please keep phones and cameras away so you can be fully present with us. Our photographer will capture every moment. After the ceremony, please feel free to take as many photos as you like and share them using our wedding hashtag!

What is the wedding hashtag?▾

Tag your posts and stories with #MartinAndBrenda2026 — we'd love to see every moment through your eyes! We'll be compiling a highlights reel from all guest photos after the wedding.

What do I do if I have dietary restrictions?▾

Please let us know when you RSVP. The caterers can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, halal, and common food allergies. No requirement is too specific — just let us know and we'll make sure you're well taken care of.

How do I get to Silang, Cavite?▾

Silang is approximately 45–60 minutes from Alabang via Sta. Rosa–Tagaytay Road (SLEX), and about 1–1.5 hours from Metro Manila depending on traffic. Grab and other ride-sharing apps are also available in the area. We recommend leaving early, especially on a Saturday afternoon.
